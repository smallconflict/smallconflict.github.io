About This Class


Advanced Interactive Storytelling and Design explores interactive storytelling from the ground up--from story inception through reporting, designing a narrative construction, production, and publication.

The "Unclass" model follows a human-centered design approach, ditching a one-size-fits-all syllabus in favor of student-centered, project-focused work. Instructors provide the structure and context through more than 50 opt-in workshops; students mapped their own path through the course.
